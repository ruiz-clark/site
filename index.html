About Me

Educator | Coder | Internationalist

As the son and grandson of Mexican fieldworkers who first came to the US without an education, Ruiz made an early decision in his career to ensure that individuals from socioeconomically disadvantaged communities have access to a quality education.

With more than a decade of global experience, Ruiz has worked across the world developing training and leadership development programs for young educators. As technology continues to become quotidian in today's modern world, Ruiz aspires to weave together his passion for education with his newfound skill set in coding.

Currently, Ruiz is a Managing Director with Teacher For America and he recently completed a coding boot camp through the University of Denver. He possesses a Master’s in International Educational Development from Columbia University and he received his teaching credentials from the University of Hawai'i at Mānoa. Ruiz graduated cum laude from Pepperdine University with a Bachelor’s degree in International Studies with a double minor in Spanish and French. In his spare time, Ruiz is currently learning Portuguese and Bahasa Malaysia.
